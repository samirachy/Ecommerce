Product description

Head out with your little one for a jog with the Strolik in the Arctic style, which features an adjustable canopy with a peek-a-boo window and covered sunroof for protection from the elements.

The handy parent tray includes two cup holders, and the covered compartment above an extra-large storage basket allows you to carry essentials on your jog.

You can connect your compatible car seat (not included) for a complete travel system, and the lightweight steel frame.
